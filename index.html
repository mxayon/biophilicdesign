Biophilic Hypotheis describes Biophilia as "The urge to affiliate with other forms of life".
Popularized by E. Wilson, the hypothesis uses data gathered from human interaction and found significant results regarding work-place productivity.

Biophilic Design Engineering promotes sustainable enviroments for nourishing innovation. Through mindful receptivity, observing design patterns of natural growth and self-evolving dynamics.

Benyus suggests looking to Nature as a "Model, Measure, and Mentor" and emphasizes sustainability as an objective of biomimicry.
